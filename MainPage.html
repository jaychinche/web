About ISTE

We Are Here to Bring Change

Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for Technical Education in the field of engineering and technology, registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. Established in 1941, ISTE aims at the career development of engineering teachers and personality development of students, contributing to the overall development of the technical education system in India.

Our Cells.

Dive into specialized excellence with our diverse cells, each offering a unique avenue for your passion and growth.


Web Master

The Web Master Cell at ISTE PCCOE is your gateway to mastering web development and design. Join us to unleash your creativity and become a web developer.

Coding Cell

The Coding Cell at ISTE PCCOE is a dynamic hub for students passionate about programming and software development. Join us to unlock your coding potential and be part of a vibrant coding community.

AI/ML

The AI/ML Cell at ISTE PCCOE explores the fascinating realms of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This cell organizes seminars and hands-on workshops that dive into Al technologies.
